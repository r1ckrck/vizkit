# fal-video custom preset

> "using @./brand.preset.md make a hero video for the homepage"

All fields are optional. Leave empty fields as `—` — Claude infers them from your request.

The structure below matches the Cinema domain preset (the most populated). For other domains (Product, Portrait, Editorial, Landscape, Abstract), copy the corresponding preset from `<project>/.claude/skills/fal-video/presets/<domain>.preset.md` instead — each has its own domain-specific fields.
